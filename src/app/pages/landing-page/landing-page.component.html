Brightlayer UI Design Patterns

The Patterns.

A design pattern is a common interaction or behavior that should be consistent across applications. In general, we follow most of the design patterns and behavior from the Material Design system. Brightlayer UI design patterns are patterns that extend/modify those from Material or are specific to Brightlayer UI applications.

While everyone is encouraged to interact with the design pattern demos to become familiar with the interactions and behaviors, this application is primarily intended for Ionic developers to provide examples of how to implement these patterns in their own applications.
